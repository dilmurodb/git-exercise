About us

OYLA Magazine has been published in
more than six languages across 10
countries since our launch in 2015. Every
issue of OYLA is made by an international
team of visionaries, educators, scientists
and science journalists.
Our goal is to introduce our young
readers to the beauty, wonder and
tremendous potential of science.
We help transform the image of science,
making it open and accessible to all.

Themen bisher nicht zu Ihren bevorzugten Interessensgebieten gezählt haben,
